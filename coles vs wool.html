Research Project

COLES vs WOOLWORTHS

Financial Performance Analysis (2019–2024)

This report compares Coles Group (Australian retail company) with its main rival Woolworths Group.

Introduction

  • Coles & Woolworths financial data from the previous three years was analysed to assess Coles’ historical performance and market position.
  • Methods included: horizontal & vertical analysis, profitability & efficiency ratios, liquidity & capital structure ratios, market performance ratios, DuPont analysis, and forecasting.

Horizontal Analysis — Key Points

  • Sales Revenue:
    • Increased from $39,369M (2022) to $43,571M (2024).
    • Growth of 2.8% in 2023 and 7.6% in 2024.
    • Driven mainly by supermarket revenue increases and slight growth in liquor sales.
    • eCommerce supermarket sales grew 30.1%, boosted by major events like Black Friday, Christmas, Easter, and Coles Online’s 25th birthday.

Vertical Analysis

  • Compares Coles’ cost structure and profitability margins over 3 years.
  • Shows stable cost of goods sold (COGS) percentages.
  • Improved gross margin in 2024.

Key Ratios

  • Profitability Ratios
    • Gross Profit Margin, Net Profit Margin, ROA, ROE (values in report).
    • Notable improvement in ROE in 2024 due to higher net income.
  • Efficiency Ratios
    • Inventory turnover and asset turnover stable, with slight efficiency gains in 2024.
  • Liquidity & Capital Structure
    • Current ratio, quick ratio indicate stable short-term liquidity.
    • Debt-to-equity ratio shows moderate leverage.
  • Market Performance Ratios
    • EPS, P/E ratio trends compared between Coles and Woolworths.

DuPont Analysis

  • Breaks ROE into profit margin, asset turnover, and equity multiplier.
  • Shows ROE improvement driven more by higher margins than leverage.

Competitor Comparison (Woolworths)

  • Woolworths maintained slightly higher sales revenue.
  • Coles outperformed in online sales growth rate.
  • Woolworths had stronger EPS and slightly higher gross margin.

Evaluation & Conclusion

  • Coles has shown consistent growth in revenue and eCommerce performance.
  • Opportunity exists to further improve operational efficiency.
  • Forecast suggests continued growth in 2025.
